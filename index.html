Meet Karen

Hi there! I'm Karen!

I'm an aspiring Full Stack Software Engineer with deep roots in the software industry - a career spanning 17 years. I've started in the industry as a Technical Writer, eventually making the transition to a Business Analyst role. My most recent, and most enjoyable, experience has been as a Product Manager for several startups in the Greater Philadelphia Area.

As a lifetime learner, I was looking to expand my skill set even further and was drawn towards software engineering. I thought "it's about time I learned how to build the things I was spending my time writing about, and then designing". I enrolled in the Penn Coding Boot Camp and started my amazing journey.

I'm looking to gain employment with an entrepreneurial company, preferably with a start-up feel (or even better, a start-up!) as a Technical Product Manager or as a Full Stack Developer. My ideal role would let me combine my love of user experience and delivering features and products that people love with my growing technical skills and prowess.

A Delco, PA native, I currently reside in Collegeville, PA with my husband, Nick, 3 dogs (Hailey, Maggie, Tank), three cats (Zipper, Frankenstein, Dexter) and a flock of eight chickens that I affectionately refer to as the "StormCoopers".
